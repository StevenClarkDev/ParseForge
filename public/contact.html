Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I get support? +
Our support team typically responds within 1 hour for chat messages and 24 hours for email inquiries. Enterprise customers receive priority support with dedicated account managers.
What support channels are available? +
We offer multiple support channels: live chat (24/7), email support, phone support for sales inquiries, comprehensive documentation, community forums, and video tutorials.
Do you offer enterprise-level support? +
Yes! Enterprise customers receive dedicated account managers, priority support with guaranteed response times, custom integration assistance, and direct access to our engineering team.
Where can I find technical documentation? +
Complete technical documentation is available in our Documentation Center. You can also try our API Playground for interactive testing.
How can I report a bug or security issue? +
For bugs, please email support@parseforge.dev with detailed steps to reproduce. For security vulnerabilities, contact security@parseforge.dev and we'll respond within 4 hours.
Can I schedule a demo or consultation? +
Absolutely! Use the contact form above or call our sales team at +1 (234) 567-890 to schedule a personalized demo or technical consultation.
