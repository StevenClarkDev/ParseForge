Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.

What happens if I exceed my limits?

We'll notify you when you approach your limit. You can either upgrade your plan or purchase additional resources as add-ons.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.

Is there a free trial?

Professional plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

How does billing work?

We bill monthly or annually based on your preference. Annual plans save 20% and are billed once per year.

Can I get a custom plan?

Yes! Enterprise customers can work with our team to create a custom plan that meets their specific needs.
